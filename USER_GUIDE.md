## üòä Mood Tracking

### How to Track Your Mood
1. Click **"Track Mood"** from Dashboard or navigate to **Mood** page
2. Select your **Mood Level** (1-10 scale):
   - 1-3: Low/Struggling
   - 4-6: Moderate/Okay
   - 7-8: Good
   - 9-10: Great/Excellent
3. Choose an **Emotion** (optional):
   - Happy, Sad, Anxious, Angry, Calm, Tired, Energetic, Neutral
4. Add a **Note** (optional): Describe what's affecting your mood
5. Click **"Save Mood"**

### Viewing Your Mood History
- See a **weekly chart** showing your mood trends
- View all past mood entries with dates
- Edit or delete previous entries
- See patterns in your emotional well-being

### Quick Mood Entry
- Use the quick mood widget on Dashboard for fast entry
- Select mood level and save instantly

## üßò Coping Strategies

### Breathing Exercise
1. Navigate to **Coping** page
2. Find the **Breathing Exercise** section
3. Click **"Start Breathing Exercise"**
4. Follow the visual guide:
   - **Breathe In**: Circle expands (4 seconds)
   - **Hold**: Pause (2 seconds)
   - **Breathe Out**: Circle contracts (4 seconds)
5. Complete 5 cycles
6. Click **"Stop"** anytime to pause

### Grounding Technique (5-4-3-2-1)
1. Scroll to **Grounding Technique** section
2. Fill in each step:
   - **5 things you can see**
   - **4 things you can touch**
   - **3 things you can hear**
   - **2 things you can smell**
   - **1 thing you can taste**
3. Complete all 5 steps
4. See completion message when done

### Mindfulness Exercises
1. Find **Mindfulness Activities** section
2. Choose an exercise:
   - Body Scan
   - Progressive Muscle Relaxation
   - Guided Meditation
   - Visualization
3. Click the exercise card
4. Follow the guided instructions
5. Complete at your own pace
